About Safenix

A modern, AI-powered emergency response system built to save lives — with a special focus on women's safety and rapid crisis response across India.

Our Mission

Making emergency response accessible to everyone

Every year, lives are lost because people don't know who to call, can't communicate their location, or freeze in the shock of an emergency. Safenix changes that.

One tap alerts contacts, shares GPS location, starts audio recording, and delivers AI guidance — all simultaneously. For women especially, these seconds matter enormously.

Our Vision

A world where no one faces an emergency alone — where technology bridges the gap between crisis and help in under three seconds.

❓ The Problem We Solve

When it matters most, simplicity saves lives

Speed

Traditional emergency response involves many steps. Safenix reduces it to one button press.

Women's Safety Gap

Women often face harassment without a discreet, fast way to call for help. Our Women Safety Hub addresses this directly.

Location Accuracy

People can't describe exact locations in a panic. GPS solves this instantly and automatically.

Guidance Gap

People don't know what to do in the minutes before help arrives. Claude AI fills this critical gap.

⚙️ Features

Everything you need in a crisis

🆘

One-Tap SOS

Crimson SOS button with GPS, contacts alert, recording, and AI in one tap.

Women Safety Mode

Dedicated women's SOS with fake call, voice activation, and audio evidence.

Claude AI Guidance

Instant AI-generated emergency instructions tailored to your situation.

Live GPS Tracking

Real-time location via Geolocation API shared with emergency contacts.

Audio Evidence

MediaRecorder API captures audio evidence the moment SOS is triggered.

Fake Call

Simulated incoming call screen to discreetly escape uncomfortable situations.

Auto-Timer SOS

Countdown timer that auto-triggers SOS if you don't cancel in time.

🌙

Dark Mode

Full dark/light theme toggle with persistent preference saved to localStorage.
