🛡️ Emergency Safety Tips

1

Stay Calm & Assess

Quickly identify the type of emergency before calling. Accurate info helps responders reach you faster.

2

Share Your Exact Location

State your exact address or landmark. Use Safenix's Location page to get GPS coordinates to share.

3

Stay on the Line

Don't hang up. Dispatchers may give critical first-aid instructions or relay your location to field teams.

4

Alert People Around You

Shout or signal to bystanders for help. In India, most people will assist someone in genuine distress.

5

Know Basic First Aid

Learn CPR and how to stop bleeding. These skills can save a life in the minutes before help arrives.

6

Prepare an Emergency Kit

Keep a first-aid box, torch, emergency cash, important documents, and a list of emergency contacts ready.
